Favorite Albums

  • Happy Hardcore/Gabber/Rave

    • Fun, energetic album featuring FORTY ONE songs from many, many artists.

      For just €10, this is a steal.

      Wonderful for parties or raves.

    • A very 'dank' album by K-orse and Basshound.

      This one's more jungle than rave, but is still rave-y.

      Chiller than others that off me nut has released, but it still goes hard.

  • Alt Rock/Breakcore

    • Head-banging, foot-stomping juggernaut of an album.

      Recommended while playing Doom or a fast-paced shooter/driving game.

      Not recommended for driving IRL, as you'll go way too fast.

  • Alternative Rap

    • This moody, experimental album is a very interesting one.

      It's an equal measure of cacophonic and rhythmic.

      Odd instuments, odd beats, and a great album.

      Some tracks have no vocals, and others have very powerful and emotional vocals.

    • An album from an artist coming from the Ukranian ghetto, this is an absolute gem by Prid Prod.

      Energetic and goofy, this album will make you bob your head even if you don't speak Russian.

  • Noise/Other

    • Feel free to grab your bow and arrows for this album.

      An extremely relaxing (or focusing) album that can be described easily by one word: "tribal".

      Beautiful rhythm layering, chilling vocals, and a diverse group of instruments make the $8 for this album worth the price.

    • Eclectic, weird, and wonderful breakcore/noise album.

      Every song cycles through peaceful calmness, frantic snare breaks, and heavy noise. Listen to this album if you want something very different, but good.

      The aural embodiment of restlessness or insomnia.

      This album is especially fun to listen to if you have ADHD or have drank too much coffee.
